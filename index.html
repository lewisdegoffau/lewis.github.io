I’m all that remains of the voices now silenced;

Sounds, once uttered, now guttered, I’ve swallowed.

While seen as a symbol of several exclusions,

Christ Jesus proclaimed I’d persist past the heavens.

And if you have not yet discovered my essence,

I’ll give you a hint: I exist in this sentence!
